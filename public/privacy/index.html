Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2025

✓  漢字 Ninja does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever.

漢字 Ninja has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that collect data. The app functions entirely offline.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your study progress locally using AsyncStorage, which is device-local storage provided by the React Native framework. This includes:

  • Your kanji review status (unseen / seen / learning / mastered)
  • Your current day-streak count
  • The date of your last study session

This data is stored only on your device. It is never transmitted anywhere. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

漢字 Ninja uses no third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services.

The app does not make any network requests during normal use. An internet connection is not required.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no personal data from anyone, it is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. It complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and equivalent regulations.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your progress data persists on your device until you delete the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

If you want to reset your progress without uninstalling, you can do so from within the app settings (if available in your version).

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy in a meaningful way, we will update the effective date above and release a new version of the app through the App Store.
